## 🌍 What it does
EcoTrace AI helps users track their daily carbon footprint and get **AI-powered, hyper-specific insights** to reduce their environmental impact — built specifically for Indian users.

## ✨ Features
- **Activity Logger** — 30+ activities across Transport, Food, Energy & Shopping with real India-specific emission factors
- **Live Dashboard** — CO₂ metrics, category breakdown bars, comparison vs India average (5.2 kg/day)
- **AI Insights** — Claude AI analyzes your actual logged data and returns 4 personalized, numbered recommendations
- **Eco Score** — A–F daily rating with streak tracker
- **7-Day Trend** — Chart.js visualization with India average line
- **Fully offline-capable** — localStorage persistence, no backend needed

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS (single file, zero dependencies)
- Chart.js for data visualization
- Claude claude-sonnet-4-6 API for AI insights
- GitHub Pages for deployment

## 📊 Emission Factors (India-specific)
| Category | Source |
|----------|--------|
| Transport | Ministry of Road Transport & Highways |
| Energy | Central Electricity Authority (0.82 kg CO₂/kWh) |
| Food | FAO global food emission factors |
| Shopping | UK DEFRA lifecycle analysis |
